LD46 - Stove Man

Posted 2020-04-21updated 2020-04-21

So, this weekend me and mr Daniel has been working like nothing else, due to Ludum Dare 46! For you who are not aquinted with the LD game jam, check out Their website

The theme for LD 46 was “Keep it alive”, which means that our game had to fit this theme mechanically. Dan remembered the old swedish comedy bit “Kaminmannen” (Stove Man), and away we went!

We had 72 hours to complete this game, from scratch (although we cheated just abit with some open source sound effects). We made a physics based game in Unity, which we tough in itself, and I had to apply my newly aquired music production skills, which are, by the way, not really up to par…
The only things which proceeded proper smooth was the modelling.

If you guys want to try out our 72 hours disasterpiece of a game, you can find it for free at Itch.io!

By the way. To you folks who volunteered for testing Goblin Insurrection, we have not forgotten you! It is taking more time than expected to make a playable (and enjoyable) demo. Hang tight, we will revisit this soon!

Until next time, stay safe!

About the Game!

Posted 2020-02-14updated 2020-02-14

So, we thought an explanation was in order, more so than "We are making a game about short green people"!

Goblin Insurrection is a puzzle game with many systemic elements! Puzzles are more similar to Legend of Zelda than the original Lemmings, but the player will use whatever items, or orders they have to control the rebel greenskins, as in Lemmings.
When traversing the caverns, to overcome obstacles you will have to operate old machines, like: lifts, drills, and bridges, to clear or make your way. You will have to burn obstacles to ash, and fill deep pits with water to swim across. Every time you finish a puzzle, we hope to deliver a genuine "aha" experience!

Apart from the classic puzzle elements, Goblin Insurrection will also feature elements of exploration, which is rewarded with loot! While the level in itself will be lit and visible, some areas will be hidden behind the fog of war.
While exploring, the goblins will find important items required to free their kin from oppression!

For the completionist, players have a reason to replay levels of Goblin Insurrection to make sure their brethren are saved.
The path to freedom is not a straight line! Found a key? Go back a few levels and replay the one with the locked door, rescue the poor sob on the other side!

These are features we have now, or those about to be implemented shortly.
As any other game developer we of course also have bigger plans. Creating a storyline, and making a map editor are only two of them, but first things first!
